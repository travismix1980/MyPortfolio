About Me

Hello, my name is Travis Mix. I have wanted to be a programmer since I was 15. Before that I wanted to be either a police officer , a pilot, and at one point a cowboy.

When I turned 15 years old, I found a c++ beginners book and enjoyed every minute I spent going through it. It was at this point I discovered what I wanted to do especially with the rest of the things I wanted to do made impossible by a combination of diabetes and epilepsy. I really did enjoy creating something from nothing that I got with programming and stuck with it from that time on till now.

My favorite programming languages are C++, and C#, and now I find myself on a journey of learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the rest of languages that make the modern internet exist. At the same time I am also learning ASP.Net Core. For fun my favorite things to do is playing board games with my family or playing the Tabletop RPG Call of Cthulhu, as well as reading. I also enjoy horror, sci-fi, and comedy movies.
